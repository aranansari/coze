Have Questions? We’re here to help

What is Coze? +
Coze is a platform designed to simplify the home-buying process for groups interested in co-buying properties. Through seamless collaboration and a suite of tools, Coze helps users find the right property, match with compatible lenders, and navigate the legal and financial aspects of co-ownership.
How does it work? +
Coze helps you and your co-buyers every step of the way, from creating an entity all the way to closing the purchase and beyond.
  1. Organize Your Group: Gather your group of co-buyers, ensuring that everyone is familiar with each other and aligned with the co-buying objectives.
  2. Form a Legal Entity: Coze assists your group in forming the appropriate legal entity, crafting contracts that reflect the unique needs and agreements of the group.
  3. Find a Property: Utilize Coze's resources to find a property that fits the needs and preferences of your group.
  4. Negotiate Financing: Coze helps your group find and negotiate with the right lender, ensuring favorable terms that align with your co-buying plan.
  5. Complete the Purchase: Finalize the purchase with the help of Coze's expert support team, knowing that all legal and financial aspects are handled with care.
  6. Manage Co-Ownership: Coze provides ongoing support for managing the co-owning relationship, handling logistics and offering tools for smooth collaboration among co-owners.
What do I need to get started? +
To get started with Coze, you'll need:
  • An active email address for registration.
  • A clear understanding of your budget and property preferences.
  • Relevant financial information if you choose to apply for financing through Coze.
  • A willingness to collaborate and communicate with potential co-buyers.
